Pick the box before the tool

Not every task deserves the same autonomy.

The expensive mistake is not choosing the wrong model. It is automating work that needs judgment, or building a complex system where a simple process fix would do.

01 / Automate

Clear and checkable

High-volume work with stable rules and low downside.

  • Classify requests
  • Extract standard fields
  • Route routine work
02 / Assist

Prepare, then decide

AI drafts or organises. A person reviews and applies judgment.

  • Reports and summaries
  • Research preparation
  • Client-response drafts
03 / Leave alone

Human by design

Rare, sensitive, ambiguous or expensive to get wrong.

  • Final professional advice
  • Employment decisions
  • Binding commitments

Questions

Before you request.

Do we need to choose an AI tool first?

No. Start with the workflow and desired result. Tool selection comes after the work, data and controls are understood.

Do we need to share company data?

Not for the initial call. Describe the process without confidential or personal information. Any later examples require explicit agreement on access and handling.

Will Voomox build something during the call?

No. The deliverable is a one-page recommendation and pilot outline, not a rushed demo.

What if AI is not the right answer?

We will say so. The recommendation may be to clarify the process, improve documentation, use simpler automation or keep the work human.

Who should join?

The person who owns the business result and someone who performs the workflow. Sensitive processes may need the relevant privacy, security or compliance owner later.
